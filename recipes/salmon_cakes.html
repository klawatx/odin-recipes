Grandma's Famous Salmon Cakes

Wondering how to make salmon cakes? This is a recipe for my grandmother's famous salmon cakes. It's a family recipe that has been passed down through generations. Serve them with macaroni and cheese or any other type of side dish.

Ingredients:

  • [14 Â¾ OZ] - Canned Salmon (drained and flaked)
  • [2 EA] - Large Eggs (beaten)
  • [1 EA] - Small Onion (diced)
  • [1 TSP] - Ground Black Pepper
  • [3 TBSP] - Vegetable Oil

Steps:

  1. Pick through salmon to remove any bones or large pieces of skin.
  2. Beat eggs in a mixing bowl. Stir in salmon, onion, and black pepper. Mix thoroughly.
  3. Shape salmon mixture into eight 2-ounce patties.
  4. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Fry patties until crispy and golden brown on the outsides, about 5 minutes per side; work in batches if necessary to avoid crowding the pan.
